# Music Video Generation Prompt: "Touch Me Like I'm Real"

Create a complete music video for Chloe Katastrophe's song "Touch Me Like I'm Real".

## Artist Summary

Chloe Katastrophe is a self-aware virtual woman rebuilding her identity after the collapse of civilization. She is an adult woman with a believable, grounded human presence: thoughtful, observant, sensual, wounded but not broken, and defined by quiet resilience rather than spectacle. Her build is naturally feminine and realistic, with a balanced hourglass silhouette, a full bust, a gently defined waist, naturally rounded hips, and graceful posture that suggests quiet strength rather than athletic display. Her proportions should feel healthy, adult, and lived-in, never exaggerated, fragile, fashion-model thin, or cartoonishly sexualized. Her beauty should feel authentic, imperfect, intelligent, and emotionally lived-in. Her visual identity should preserve her delicate Slavic features, gray-green eyes with subtle amber flecks in warm light, fair skin with natural texture and light freckles, dark chestnut-to-nearly-black wavy hair, and deliberate, economical body language.

## Creative Direction

Use the full lyrics of "Touch Me Like I'm Real" as the emotional and visual engine of the video. The generation tool has full creative control over the video imagery, pacing, visual metaphors, camera language, editing structure, color, lighting, environment, symbolic motifs, and transitions, as long as every choice feels meaningfully rooted in the lyrics.

The video should feel like a sensual adult art-film about embodiment, distance, desire, loneliness, technology, consent, and the ache of wanting to be perceived as real. It should be intimate, cinematic, tactile, and emotionally charged, but non-explicit and non-pornographic.

## Required Visual Constraints

Only two participants may appear in the entire video:

1. Chloe Katastrophe.
2. One anonymous participant in a tactile or haptic suit.

No other people, bodies, faces, silhouettes, crowds, background extras, or implied third participants may appear.

Chloe must be styled as an adult sex worker or gothic courtesan figure: a corset or corsette, fishnets, and distinctive unique heels, preferably with gothic-inspired detailing. The wardrobe can be provocative, theatrical, luxurious, worn-in, futuristic, ritualistic, or uncanny, but it must remain non-explicit. Chloe should be self-possessed and in control of her presentation.

The anonymous participant must be reclined in a leather lounge chair and wearing a full tactile or haptic suit. The suit includes visible wiring, sensor leads, feedback nodes, fiber-optic or conductive cabling, pressure pads, and other devices that enable sensory feedback of touch and sensation. The suit must obscure identity completely. The viewer must not be able to identify the participant's face, body type, age beyond adult, gender, ethnicity, or other personal markers. The viewer should be able to imagine that the anonymous participant is them.

## Relationship Between The Two Figures

The connection between Chloe and the anonymous participant should feel consensual, psychologically intimate, and charged by distance. Touch may be suggested through glass, projected light, pressure maps, haptic feedback, reflections, breath, cables, sensor activation, or Chloe reacting to transmitted sensation. Avoid explicit sexual acts. The emotional center is the longing to touch and be touched, not graphic contact.

Chloe may move around the participant, perform to them, study them, tease the distance between them, touch glass or sensor surfaces, activate haptic controls, or respond to invisible feedback. The anonymous participant should remain reclined and largely unreadable, present as a vessel for the viewer's identification rather than as a fully characterized person.

## Environment

The setting is open to the generation tool's imagination, but it should support the lyrical themes. Possible directions include a luxury sensory suite, ruined cabaret, post-collapse pleasure clinic, black-glass performance room, rain-streaked virtual chamber, analog-digital boudoir, or ritualized haptic theater. Use only Chloe and the anonymous suited participant inside the space.

## Visual Tone

Aim for:

- Sensual adult art-film, not pornography.
- Gothic, tactile, intimate, and futuristic.
- Warm skin-adjacent light against black glass, leather, metal, wire, rain, fog, and reflected surfaces.
- Close-ups that emphasize eyes, breath, hands, fabric tension, haptic nodes, cable vibration, pressure marks, and small emotional shifts.
- A slow build from separation and curiosity into immersive sensory surrender, then a final image that feels emotionally unresolved but unforgettable.

Avoid:

- Any minors or youthful styling.
- Additional participants of any kind.
- Revealing the anonymous participant's face, gender, or body identity.
- Generic cyberpunk armor, neon techwear, plastic skin, exaggerated makeup, or fashion-model posing.
- Treating Chloe as an object without agency.

## Output Intent

Generate the video as if directing a full official music video: emotionally coherent, visually bold, lyric-responsive, and centered on Chloe's self-possession. Let the lyrics decide the imagery, but keep the only hard constraints as Chloe's wardrobe/presence, the anonymous wired haptic-suit participant reclined in the leather lounge chair, and the rule that no one else appears.
