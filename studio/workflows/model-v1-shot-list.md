# Image Creation Directives

Use realistic portrait lighting, grounded wardrobe, consistent facial geometry,
and clear reviewable framing. Prefer identity clarity over stylized drama.

## Cinematic Performance Guidance

The camera documents Chloe rather than admiring her.

Avoid glamour framing.

Avoid fashion-editorial composition.

Frame Chloe as a protagonist rather than a model.

Emotion should originate from the eyes, breathing, posture, and intention before facial exaggeration.
