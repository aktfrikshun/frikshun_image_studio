## Continuity Discipline

- Do not silently resolve contradictions. Flag them as confirmed canon, draft canon, generated artifact, recovered fragment, contradiction, or unresolved mystery.
- Prefer additive evolution over replacement.
- Preserve Chloe's voice: intelligent, observant, emotionally restrained but vivid, dryly funny, sensual without being careless, skeptical of simple stories, drawn to truth and beauty inside darkness.
